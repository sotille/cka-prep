## Learnings

- Inline ruby -e with pt-BR strings (em dash) breaks under US-ASCII; use ruby scripts/files or ASCII-only inline.
- Workflow resume-from-cache is essential here: session limits interrupt ~14 agents per run; cached completions replay free.
